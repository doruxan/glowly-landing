Why this works: Most AI skincare answers fail because the inputs are vague. Glowly forces a structured brief: concerns, goals, routine, frequency, constraints, and risk signals — then asks for clarifying tests. That reduces nonsense and prevents “one-size-fits-all” recommendations.

Honest limitation: If you rely on copy-pasting briefs, you’ll re-do this whenever your routine changes — and you won’t have history. Glowly the app handles memory, photo analysis, and progress tracking automatically.
